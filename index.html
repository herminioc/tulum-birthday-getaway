Getting Around Tulum

🛵
Scooter

Villa → beach: 5–10 min
Villa → downtown: 10–15 min

  • Always wear a helmet
  • Never ride after drinking
  • Photograph before accepting
  • Confirm insurance & fuel
🚕
Taxis
  • Agree on fare before getting in
  • Carry pesos & small bills
  • Ask villa to call a trusted driver
  • Prices run higher in evenings
📱
Uber & App Rides

Uber availability varies in Tulum. Taxis or prearranged drivers are more dependable for scheduled plans. Rideshare prices tend to be higher during peak evening hours.

Emergency · Mexico
911
Police, fire, or medical — dial 911 anywhere in Mexico.
  • Drink bottled water only — tap water is not safe for drinking
  • Use insect repellent, especially at dawn and dusk
  • Currency is Mexican Peso (MXN) — carry cash and small bills
  • Tipping: 15–20% at restaurants is standard
  • July is warm and humid — brief afternoon showers are common
  • If anyone feels unwell, rest, hydrate, and let the group know
